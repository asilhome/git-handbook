## Group

### Create a New Group

Reason to create a group is it has access permission that you can control.
Access: To create a group only by user who has **Can create group** enabled.
Give it a name and click on **Create Group**.

![New Group](images/gitlab-group-create.png)

A new group is created.

![New Group Created](images/gitlab-group-created.png)

And notified by Email.

![New Group Created Notified](images/gitlab-email-group-created.png)
